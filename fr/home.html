Le Pestou is a converted farmhouse with six bedrooms, lost in the rolling countryside of Bas-Quercy in the Southwest of France

The house is secluded and peaceful, with lovely views. There are 6 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms +2 wc's, a large salon with a gallery, a ping pong room, a heated swimming pool with outdoor shower, a terrain de boules and a badminton lawn. Toulouse is an hour away, with flights from all over Europe. The TGV to Paris is half an hour away. Bordeaux is two hours.

This part of France is away from the tourist routes, about 2 ½ hours from the Mediterranean, the Atlantic, and the Pyrenees. There are pretty market towns within a few minutes' drive, as well as many charming villages with good cafés and restaurants. The vineyards of Cahors are not far away.
