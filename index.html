Le Pestou is a converted farmhouse, lost in the rolling countryside of Bas-Quercy in the Southwest of France, one hour from Toulouse and two hours from Bordeaux.

It is secluded and peaceful, with lovely views, and has 6 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms +2 wc's, a large salon with a gallery, a ping pong room, a heated swimming pool with outdoor shower, a terrain de boules and a badminton lawn.

This part of France is away from the tourist routes, about 2 ½ hours from the Mediterranean, the Atlantic, and the Pyrenees. There are pretty market towns within a few minutes' drive, as well as many charming villages with good cafés and restaurants. The vineyards of Cahors are not far away.
